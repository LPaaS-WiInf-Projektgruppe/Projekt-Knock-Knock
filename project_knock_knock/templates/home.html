Why?

Every day a huge amount of people, packages, materials, food etc. is moved around. Unfortuantly often the involved people are not communicating to coordinate accordingly and efficiently. This creates an insufficient possibility to react to fluctuations in demand on both ends. To solve this issue we developed "KnockKnock". The application provides a possibility to share resources and there reduces redundant rides.
